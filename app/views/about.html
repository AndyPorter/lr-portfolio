About DWP Digital Services

The digital portfolio belongs to the Department for Work & Pensions (DWP) Business Transformation Group (BTG).

How do things get onto the digital portfolio?

To get a new service on the portfolio contact the digital portfolio lead, James Johnson.

  1. The Portfolio Team will help you define the problem you want to solve and consult over 30 colleagues across DWP for feedback.
  2. Following the consultation stage we will finalise an Discovery Approval Document.
  3. We will then present the problem to the BTG MTM who decide whether to approve discovery.
  4. If discovery is approved it's prioritised by the BTG management team and added to the portfolio backlog. Priorities are ratified by the Business Design Authority.
  5. The items in the backlog are mobilised in order of priority as soon as they have the right people for the task, as determined by the Challenge and Support Forum.
